Welcome to the fan page of Manchester City Football Club!

Founded in 1880 as St. Mark's (West Gorton), they became Ardwick Association Football Club in 1887 and Manchester City in 1894. One of the oldest football clubs playing in the Premier League at the moment. Join our fan society; let's support the club together.
